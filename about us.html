About us

Established in the year 1998, the Meridian Resort and Club is a bastion of luxury and elegance. Located in the southern part of Pune, the resort is set like a jewel amidst 25 acres of lush green carefully manicured gardens. The GRECO-EYGPTIAN styled resort is a showcase of luxurious relaxation with tropically-inspired designs, grand architecture, a sun-drenched atrium, wide shaded corridors and flower lined patios for a memorable stay.

The exquisite architecture seamlessly blends with the landscape, laying open an unbound world of rejuvenation. It will not be surprising if you find yourself absorbed in the fine particulars of nature, jewel-like details that would otherwise have evaded your attention. For senses dulled by the rigors of the city, the resorts soothing environs will prove out to be a welcome break.
